Resources and other ways to help!

These are a few helpful resources and unique ways to help the effort. List is a work in progress.

Natl Resource List

A huge spreadsheet of resources to support the blm movement originally created by @BotanicalDyke. Includes info on jail support and legal services by state, tips on effective protesting and social media, other innovative low and zero cost ways to help, and much more.

Spreadsheet

blacklivesmatters.carrd.co

Includes a comprehensive list of petitions, phone numbers to text/call to start a dialogue, and ways to donate directly to victims, protestors, and black owned businesses.

Website

Crisis Text Line

Provides free 24/7 support via text message. Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a Crisis Counselor. Please please seek this out if you're anxious or afraid. This is a confidential service backed by many volunteers. These are trying times so don't be nervous about getting help or starting a dialogue.

Indie Game Bundle for Racial Justice and Equality

A bundle hosted by itch.io with content from 1000+ indie game creators. It is pay what you want with minimum donation of $5 and all proceeds will be donated to the NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund and Community Bail Fund split 50/50.

Website Bundle

EatOkra App

A guide to finding black-owned restaurants and food trucks in your area. Available on the Apple app store and the Google Play Store.
