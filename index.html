BLM Bay Area Fundraiser

Donations for Personalized Playlists!

Welcome!

This is a fundraiser for the Black Lives Matter movement and 100% of the funds will go towards local Bay Area organizations that support Black communities and protestors. For $3 each, we'll personalize a playlist to your musical tastes!

Bay Area Organizations

This is a list of organizations you can choose to donate to!

Bay Area Black Owned Business Relief Fund

A relief fund for Bay Area owned Black business owners affected by the protests, riots, and looting.

GoFundMe: https://www.gofundme.com/f/bay-area-black-owned-business-relief-fund

Bay Area Bail + Protestor Fund

A fund for supplies that will keep protestors safe, hospital bills, things that will amplify their noise, transportation for protestors, bail relief, and other necessary resources.

GoFundMe: https://www.gofundme.com/f/bay-area-bail-fund

People's Breakfast Oakland

A Black grassroot organization serving the people of Oakland by providing meals and packages to houseless communities.

Website: https://www.hellablackpod.com/pbo
Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/peoplesbreakfastoakland/?hl=en

Black Earth Farmers

A Grassroots Pan-African + Pan-Indigenous farming collective that delivers free food to Black people who have been arrested, bailed, and injured from protests in the East Bay.

Website: https://www.blackearthfarms.com/
Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/blackearthfarms/?hl=en

National Lawyer's Guild - SF Chapter

A lawyer's guild that provides pro-bono legal support for folks that have been arrested in the Bay Area.

Website: https://nlgsf.org/ Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/nlgsf/
Twitter: https://twitter.com/nlgsf?lang=en
